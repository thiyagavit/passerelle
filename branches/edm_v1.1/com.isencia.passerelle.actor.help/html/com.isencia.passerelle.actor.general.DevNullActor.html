DevNullActor

com.isencia.passerelle.actor.general.DevNullActor

An actor that discards all incoming msgs. Useful to e.g. link to an error-output of one or more actors, to implement an "ignore error" behaviour. It can be configured to completely ignore all incoming messages, or alternatively to just log them in the log files.
